Pau Gasol aka E.T

The man who put Spain in the center of the Basketball world.

Pau Gasol timeline carrer:

  • FC Barcelona (1999-2001) "Wins the Spanish league championship"
  • Memphis Grizzlies (2001-2008) "First time Memphis playoffs, wins the 2006 basketball world cup and the 2008 O.G silver medal"
  • Los Angeles Lakers (2008-2014) "Wins two NBA championship and the 2012 O.G silver medal"
  • Chicago Bulls (2014-2016) "Reach the NBA playoffs twice and the 2016 O.G bronze medal"
  • San Antonio Spurs (2016-2019)
  • Milwaukee Bucks (2019)
  • Portland Trail Blazers (2019)
  • FC Barcelona (2021)
